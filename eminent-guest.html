Hon'ble Rajnath Singh Ji, Defence Minister

Rajnath Singh ji born 10th July 1951, is an Indian politician and lecturer who is serving the nation as 29th Defence Minister of India since 2019. He visited IMRT campus on the occasion of Makar Sankranti.He is currently deputy leader of the hose, Lok Sabha since 2014. He is a veteran leader of BHARITYA JANTA PARTY who started his career a RSS Swayamsevak.

Sudha Singh, An International Athlete

Sudha Singh (born 25 June 1986) is an Indian Olympic athlete in the 3000 metres steeplechase event. A national record holder in the event, she has represented India at international events since 2005. Singh's breakthrough came at the 2010 Asian Games in Guangzhou, where she won the gold medal in steeplechase. She has since won a gold at the 2017 Asian Athletics Championships in Bhubaneswar and a silver at the 2018 Asian Games in Jakarta and has represented India at two consecutive Olympic Games in 2012 and 2016. Singh was conferred with India's second highest sporting honor, the Arjuna Award in 2012.She was awarded India's fourth highest civilian award the Padma Shri in 2021.

Navneet Sehgal, An Inspirational personlity

Navneet Kumar Sehgal (born 25 July 1963) is an Indian Administrative Services officer of 1988 batch, currently serving as Additional Chief Secretary, sports and youth welfare in the Government of Uttar Pradesh. He also served as Additional Chief Secretary Information, MSME ,Khadi & Village Industry in the state. He is also a qualified Chartered Accountant. He has served as District Magistrate of Gonda, Ayodhya, Varanasi and Lucknow.
