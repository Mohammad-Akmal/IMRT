Sports

IMRT Sports Club & Hostel Sports Club

The Sports Club at IMRT business school provides facilities for a variety of sports, including football, cricket, basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, and athletics. The Club also hosts regular tournaments and events, allowing students to compete and interact with each other. The Sports Club has trained coaches who provide guidance and support to students, helping them to improve their skills and techniques.

Table Tennis

Students at IMRT business school can use the Table Tennis room to practice their skills individually or compete against each other. The college also conducts regular Table Tennis tournaments and events, providing students with an opportunity to showcase their talent and compete with their peers.

Carrom

The Carrom room at IMRT business school is an essential part of the college's infrastructure, providing students with an opportunity to engage in indoor games and activities. The Carrom room is well-equipped, providing a comfortable and conducive environment for students to practice and compete in this exciting game.

Volleyball

Students at IMRT business school can use the Volleyball court to practice their skills individually or compete against each other. The college also conducts regular Volleyball tournaments and events, providing students with an opportunity to showcase their talent and compete with their peers.

Tug Of War

Tug of War is a traditional sport that is enjoyed by students at IMRT business school. The college has a dedicated Tug of War area that provides students with an opportunity to practice and compete in this exciting sport.

Chess

Students at IMRT business school can use the Chess room to practice their skills individually or compete against each other. The college also conducts regular Chess tournaments and events, providing students with an opportunity to showcase their talent and compete with their peers.

Kabaddi

The Kabaddi ground at IMRT business school is well-maintained, providing a safe and comfortable environment for students to play in. The ground is equipped with high-quality Kabaddi mats and other equipment, ensuring that students can play the game to their best abilities.

Winners Of Sports Events

Overall, the college's sports events and competitions provide students with an opportunity to showcase their talent, compete with their peers, and foster a culture of sportsmanship and teamwork. The winners of these events are recognized for their hard work and dedication, inspiring other students to pursue sports and physical activities.

Kho Kho Team

The Kho Kho team is an integral part of the college's sports culture. The college has a dedicated Kho Kho ground where students can practice and compete in this exciting sport.

Cricket

The college has a well-equipped cricket ground, which provides a safe and comfortable environment for students to practice and compete in the sport. The ground is equipped with high-quality equipment such as cricket balls, bats, and protective gear, ensuring that students can play the game to their best abilities.

Football Team

The college has a dedicated football team comprising of talented and enthusiastic students who are passionate about the sport. The team has a well-experienced coach who provides guidance and support to students, helping them to improve their skills and techniques.

Badminton Team

The college conducts regular practice matches and coaching sessions, enabling students to hone their skills and techniques. The badminton team also participates in various inter-college and district-level tournaments, providing students with an opportunity to showcase their talent and compete with students from other colleges and universities.

Winner Players Of Volleyball Team

The college is proud of the volleyball team's achievements and recognizes the hard work and dedication of each player. The college felicitates the winning players, acknowledging their contribution to the college's sports culture and inspiring other students to pursue their passion for sports.

Gold Medalist

We applaud the hard work and dedication of these students, who have not only made us proud but also served as an inspiration to other students. Their success reinforces our commitment to nurturing talent and providing students with opportunities to excel in their respective fields
